ABOUT US

Al-Ameen College, Edathala, Aluva is an pioneering Arts and Science Co-Educational Institution of higher education under aided sector. It is one of the flagship institutions run by the Al-Ameen Education Trust, Kochi. The Trust is part of the Al-Ameen Movement established in Bangalore by a group of dedicated, inspired thinkers and professionals who wished to impart education among the minority sections of the society. The Founder of Al-Ameen Movement, Dr.Mumthaz Ahamed Khan, is a visionary who strove to make his dreams reality. His innate integrity and compassion compelled him towards the development of the downtrodden sections of society. He visualized three major areas for effective socio-economic uplift; namely, education, health care and banking. The realisation of this thought led to the establishment of the Al-Ameen Movement and its numerous institutions of excellence.
